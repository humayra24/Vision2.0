Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives Bangladesh forward. Startups, tech hubs, and smart ideas reshape industries daily.

Sustainability

Sustainability guides Bangladesh’s growth—green energy, farming, and climate action lead the way

Infrastructure

Bangladesh builds modern infrastructure—bridges, highways, and tech zones rapidly powering future progress

Empowerment

Empowerment shapes Bangladesh—youth, women, and innovation are driving change and opportunity

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Poverty is unnecessary. To overcome poverty and the flaws of the economic crisis in our society, we need to envision our social life. We have to free our mind, imagine what has never happened before and write social fiction. We need to imagine things to make them happen. If you don't imagine, it will never happen. All human beings are born entrepreneurs. Some get a chance to unleash that capacity. Some never got the chance, never knew that he or she has that capacity. The developing world is full of entrepreneurs and visionaries, who with access to education, equity and credit would play a key role in developing the economic situations in their countries."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
